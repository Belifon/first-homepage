Scratch Project: Maze


Project link on scratch.mit.edu

Instructions

  1. To progress to the next level, guide the ball to the green box. Use the arrow keys or on-screen arrows (for mobile) to navigate through the maze. Aim for 3 points with 0 hits, or just enjoy playing!
  2. Need some help? Hover over the gray box at the bottom of the screen to open the tips menu.
  3. There are three levels to play by default. To switch between them, click one of the three numbers at the top right or use the keys 1, 2, or 3 on your keyboard. Want to find a bonus level? The chick can help you get there!
  4. Click the ball to change its color, and press SPACE to reset its position. To reset the counters, click the restart button on the bottom right. To reset all progress, press R or 0.
